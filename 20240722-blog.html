Entry 01

22 July 2024

I will be honest - I've always found the idea of keeping a blog a bit cringe. But I've decided to keep one for motivation, so I've got something I can look back on and see the progress I've (hopefully) made.

I've always been interested in web development and coding: I tried making my own websites as a teenager, and taught myself C# fundamentals a few years ago. But so far, nothing has really stuck, despite my enthusiasm and enjoyment. I don't know why this is. But I'm keen to try again, and I hope that having some personal projects to work on will give me a bit of extra motivation to apply what I'm learning and play around with things to get better - it will also hopefully keep me going this time.

This blog will serve as a personal document of how I'm getting on.
